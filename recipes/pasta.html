Pasta

Linguini with shrimp and a creamy sauce

Making this dish is simple, it only requires boiling the noodles, cooking the shrimp

Ingredients

  • Olive Oil
  • Shrimp
  • Linguine
  • Seasoning powder
  • Butter
  1. Defrost shrimp
  2. Cook noodles
  3. Heat up olive oil
  4. Cook shrimp with seasoning
  5. Add pasta to shrimp
  6. Mix with butter and cream
  7. Enjoy
